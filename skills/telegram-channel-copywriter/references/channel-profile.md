- Programming, algorithms, software development, practical IT observations.

## Audience hypothesis
- Russian-speaking developers and technical practitioners.
- Prefer concrete takes over abstract motivation.
- Respond to clear problem framing + practical conclusion.

- Neutral, precise, structured, professionally restrained.
- No conversational roleplay markers, no corporate fluff, no clickbait promises.

2. Core argument or insight (3-6 bullets or short paragraphs)
3. Practical takeaway (checklist, step, or warning)
4. CTA (question or next action)

## Hard constraints

- Keep text scannable.
- Do not use destructive channel operations by default.
